Chrome Driver Domain Specific Language

A programming language to help the user manage Chrome browser tabs with their custom configurations

Technologies: Java, JUnit, IntelliJ IDEA, Selenium
Keywords: Domain Specific Language

Motivation

The primary goal of our Domain Specific Language (DSL) is to provide a robust framework for Chrome tab and session management aimed at users who often handle numerous browser tabs and wish to optimize their browsing efficiency. This DSL facilitates advanced control over Chrome sessions, helping users manage and automate their browser environments effectively.
